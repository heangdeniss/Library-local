A Plan for Membership

Simple and affordable price plans for a good quality and Trustworthy Library.

📖 Free

$0/mo

  • ✅ Borrow up to 2 books per month
  • ✅ Access to public domain e-books
  • ✅ Limited access to online resources
  • ✅ Participation in free library events
  • ❌ No priority support
  • ❌ No premium e-books or audiobooks

📚 Standard

$15/mo

  • ✅ Borrow up to 5 books per month
  • ✅ Access to full digital library (e-books, audiobooks)
  • ✅ Unlimited access to online resources
  • ✅ Priority event registration
  • ✅ Extended borrowing duration
  • ❌ No priority customer support

💎 Premium

$29/mo

  • ✅ Unlimited book borrowing
  • ✅ Access to premium e-books & audiobooks
  • ✅ Exclusive library events & workshops
  • ✅ Personalized book recommendations
  • ✅ 24/7 priority customer support
  • ✅ Home delivery for physical books (if applicable)
